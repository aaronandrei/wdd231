The Backview Heights Site Plan

Site Name

The site name is "The Backview Heights."

Optional domain availability: thebackviewheights.com

Site Purpose

The site is to provide the band, "The Backview Heights" with an online presence to keep followers of the band up to date with upcoming shows, band information, music and newsletter enrollment.

Scenarios

Where can I listen to the bands music?

When and where is the band performing?

Color Schema

The color schema will consist of:

  • Black
  • Red
  • Yellow
  • Orange
  • Beige

The main colors will be black, red and beige for most of the design with yellow and red adding accents.

Typography

The main font will be "Open Sans" which will be used for most paragraphs and sections. "Special Gothic Expanded One" will be used only for the h1 heading.
